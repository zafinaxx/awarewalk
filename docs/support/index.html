Frequently Asked Questions

What devices does AwareWalk support?
AwareWalk is designed for Apple Vision Pro running visionOS 2.0 or later.
How do I activate the HUD?
Open AwareWalk and tap the Guardian Eye button on the main screen. The app will enter mixed immersive mode, overlaying safety information in your field of vision.
Does AwareWalk collect my data?
No. All data (location, spatial awareness, hand tracking) is processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
How do I manage my subscription?
Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to view, modify, or cancel your AwareWalk subscription.
How do I restore my purchases?
Open AwareWalk > Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore all previously purchased themes and subscriptions.
The HUD is not showing navigation data. What should I do?
Make sure you have granted AwareWalk permission to access your location. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services and enable access for AwareWalk.
